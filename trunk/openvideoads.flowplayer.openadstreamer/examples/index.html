Flowplayer Open Ad Streamer - Worked Examples

Zone Definitions

The worked examples assume that a series of "zones" have been defined in the OpenX server as follows:

Linear: Pre, Mid and Post-Roll Videos

  • zone '1': RTMP MP4 Linear Video
  • zone '4': RTMP MP4 Linear Video with Companion Ad
  • zone '5': HTTP MP4 Linear Video
  • zone '6': HTTP MP4 Linear Video with Companion Ad
  • zone '13': RTMP FLV Linear Video
  • zone '16': HTTP FLV Linear Video
  • zone '14': RTMP MP4 Linear Video with 'Click to Website'
  • zone '17': HTTP MP4 Linear Video with 'Click to Website'
  • zone '15': Mixed of HTTP and RTMP MP4 Linear Video
Non-Linear: Overlays
  • zone '2': Overlay - HTML click to play RTMP MP4 RTMP video
  • zone '12': Overlay - HTML click to play HTTP MP4 video
  • zone '3': Overlay - HTML click to go to the advertiser website
  • zone 'X': Overlay - Text click to go advertiser website
  • zone 'X': Overlay - SWF only click to go to advertiser website
  • zone 'X': Overlay - Image click to go advertiser website
