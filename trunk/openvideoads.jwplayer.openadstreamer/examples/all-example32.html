Example 32 - Delivering Image Overlay Ads

This example delivers image overlays at the bottom of the player screen. This example uses the reserved region "reserved-bottom-w450px-h50px-transparent"

A COMPANION ADVERTISEMENT WILL APPEAR IN HERE

The configuration for this example is: 


<embed 
  id="ply"
  data="../dist/4.5.swf"
  src="../dist/4.5.swf" 
  width="450"
  height="300"
  allowscriptaccess="always"
  allowfullscreen="true"
  flashvars="height=300&width=450&plugins=../dist/OpenAdStreamer.swf&config=config/all32.xml"
/>
